🦃 Southwest Idaho - Turkey Hunt Insights
Interactive scouting & planning map for GMUs 32, 32A, 33, 38 & 39
Welcome! This map is built to help you scout and plan your Idaho turkey hunt. Use the panels at the bottom right to explore layers, read the legend, and access tools like weather, markers, and draw & measure. Click anything on the map to learn more about it.
🗓️ Seasons
  • Use the Spring / Fall buttons at the bottom to switch seasons - GMU boundaries, hunt types, dates, and sex restrictions all update automatically
  • Hunt types shown: General (open to all), Controlled (draw tag), Youth
  • Click any GMU on the map to see season dates, sex restrictions, and a direct link to the 2026–27 regulations
🗂️ Map Layers
  • 📋 Regulations - GMU boundaries colored by hunt type and IDFG Regional Offices; click any unit for season details and a link to the official IDFG regs
  • 🔓 Access - AccessYes! properties, WMAs, public land ownership, trails, roads, and campgrounds to help plan your hunt
  • 🦃 Turkey Habitat - Burned areas, logging clearings, deciduous forest, cropland, snow cover, turkey range, and water; stack these to find high-probability spots
🖱️ Click Any Feature
  • Every layer is interactive - click for details, scouting tips, and acreage
  • GMU popups include a link to the official IDFG regulations for that unit
📍 My Markers
  • Open 🛠️ Tools, pick a marker type (Turkey, Sign, Roost, Camp, etc.), then click the map to drop a pin
  • Add notes and save - markers persist on your device between visits
🛠️ Tools
  • 🌤️ Weather - Click the map then open Weather for a live 7-day NWS forecast at that exact location
  • 📐 Draw & Measure - Draw lines (distance in miles) or polygons (area in acres) to plan routes and setups
  • 🗺️ Basemap - Switch between Satellite, Outdoors, and Topo in the Layers panel
⚠️ Always verify current regulations before heading into the field. Regulations, season dates, and area closures can change. Access the official regs directly from any GMU popup on the map.
